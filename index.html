Why South East Queensland — and why now

Greater Brisbane remains undersupplied, with strong population growth and constrained new builds. With the 2032 Olympics catalysing infrastructure and ongoing migration from southern states, quality lifestyle properties are expected to remain in demand.

  • Low comparable stock in the acreage segment
  • Strong lifestyle migration to SEQ (value vs Sydney/Melbourne)
  • Improving buyer sentiment as rates ease
